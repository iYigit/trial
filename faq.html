FAQ

I decided to create a simple FAQ page to make some common questions I always get a little bit clear.

What the hell is ''Jailbreak''?


Well, Jailbreaking is the process by which full execute and write access is obtained on all the partitions of iOS, iPadOS, tvOS and watchOS. It's usually done by patching /private/etc/fstab to mount the System partition as 'read-write'.


Even it sounds really cool, is it legal?

Hey, I've a nice news for you, yes it is! It's legal*. But as I always say, such beautiful things come with some risks. 

*1 Depends on your country/region. 

Does it void the warranty?

Do you wanna get an honest answer? Well, yes, it does. But don't panic. You can safely remove the jailbreak and everything related to it with some easy steps. 
And here's a real life example: While you go to an Apple store (for whole Turkey, there are a few Apple stores in Istanbul only), they cannot find it. And yeah, most of the time
they don’t even care to check if it's jailbroken or not :P 

Sounds promising! Could you show me how to Jailbreak my iDevice?

Sure. For an average user, there are some nicely created apps/GUIs to jailbreak an iDevice with no coding experience.
