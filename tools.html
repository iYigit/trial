Jailbreak Tools

Just a list of popular tools that allow you to jailbreak your iDevice without any coding experience.

Checkra1n

Checkra1n is a community project to provide a high-quality semi-tethered jailbreak to all, based on the ‘checkm8’ bootrom exploit.

Unc0ver

Unc0ver, one of the most preferred jailbreak tool, allows you to operate your iDevice in your way.

Odyssey

Odyssey, an open-source jailbreak tool, which gives your device freedom.
